This post shows how to add bibliography to simple blog posts. We support every citation style that jekyll-scholar does. That means simple citation like (L’Yi et al., 2015), multiple citations like (L’Yi et al., 2015; L’Yi et al., 2019), long references like L’Yi, S., Chang, Y., Shin, D. H., & Seo, J. (2019). Toward Understanding Representation Methods in Visualization Recommendations through Scatterplot Construction Tasks. Computer Graphics Forum, 38(3), 201–211. https://doi.org/10.1111/cgf.13682 or also quotes:
